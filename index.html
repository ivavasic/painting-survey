Welcome to the Painting Evaluation Study!

This survey is intended for research purposes and involves ranking the selected paintings from the Getty Collection. These rankings will serve as a baseline to evaluate the performance of Large Language Models on the same tasks.

Context:

You are a painting evaluator. Please imagine that the questions or requirements you receive represent the needs or inquiries of visitors to a virtual museum. Your task is to rank each painting according to how well it fulfills these visitor requirements. Evaluate each painting individually based on the presented questions, without comparing it to other paintings.

Your task:

Rank each painting from 1 to 5 based on how well it meets the userâ€™s requirement:

  1. Does not meet the criteria or relevant information is missing from the description.
  2. Meets very few aspects.
  3. Meets some aspects.
  4. Meets most aspects.
  5. Meets entirely the criteria.
If the information needed to answer a question is missing from the painting's metadata or description (e.g., description, place of creation, or other relevant details), assign a rank of 1.

Note 1: You will see one painting per page. Please answer all 20 requests before continuing.
Note 2: Each painting consists mostly of metadata information followed by the description of the painting, while a few of them involve metadata only. The metadata and the description appear as separate paragraphs.
Note 3: The content of the painting is entirely copied from the Getty Collection website. Some text may appear unstructured due to the formatting.
